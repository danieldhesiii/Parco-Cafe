01 Our story

Made fresh,
served with
a smile.

We're a family-run café tucked against the green edge of Vauxhall Park. Mornings start with the hiss of the coffee machine and the smell of fresh pastries; by lunch the counter fills with hot plates, hearty specials and sandwiches stacked the way they should be.

Nothing here is rushed and nothing is pretending to be more than it is — good ingredients, cooked properly, served by people who'll remember your order next time.
